  • 01 What is FoSCos / FSSAI Food License?

    FSSAI launched Food Safety Compliance System (FoSCoS) wef 1st June 2020 replacing existing Food Licensing and Registration System.Food License Registration certificate is required to run a food product business. You have to register under Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) to manufacture, distribute and transport food products. FSSAI is established under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India. The FSSAI has been established under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. FSSAI Certificate and FSSAI License are same which ensures the quality, purity and other important factors the customers can rely upon.

  • 02 What is the difference between FSSAI/FoSCoS/Food License?

    There is no difference between FSSAI, FoSCoS and Food License. These are all the same.

Who Needs a FSSAI FoSCos License?

Documents required for new FSSAI registration/licence

  • Passport-size photo
  • Address proof of business premises (rental agreement, a letter of authorisation from the owner of the rented premises, utility bills, and so on)
  • List of food products manufactured or processed
  • Plan for a food safety management system
  • Any Identity Proof documents like Aadhaar Card, Ration Card, Voter ID Card, PAN Card, or Driving License.
  • Certificate of the business constitution, such as a partnership deed, certificate of incorporation, shop and establishment licence, or other business registration certificate.
